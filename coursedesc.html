Course Description

This course will teach you how to create a variety of origami objects, including swans, flowers etc.

We have 45-minute classes of up to five students for people of all ages and levels, from those who have never folded before to seasoned veterans looking for a new challenge. Our staff has over 50 combined years teaching origami, and our flexible schedule ensures that we can find a time that works for you.

Our classes are designed around our brand new “Kyu” teaching method. We have taken all the folds needed in origami and categorized them by difficulty so they are easy to follow and fun to learn. Like martial arts, the Kyu System relies on progression through practice, repetition, and demonstration. By displaying mastery of a specific set of techniques, students will receive colored bands as they ‘graduate’ to new levels; in the same manner that belts are awarded in karate.

Requirements

*No prior experience is necessary but we strongly recommend that students be at least five years of age to take Origami Classes. It is a shared class environment so it is important that each folder can listen to and follow directions for 45 minutes.
